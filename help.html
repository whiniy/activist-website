How You can Help

1. Shop from sustainable and ethical fashion companies.

2. Donate, sell, recycle, or repurpose old clothes instead of trashing it.

3. Buy secondhand clothing or try thrifting.

4. Buy less low quality, cheap garments and more high quality clothes made with non-toxic materials.

5. Consider renting clothes that you know you will only wear a few times.
